RK STORE

Buy Stationery Items and Groceries

Working hours: 8:30 AM - 7:30 PM (including Sundays)

At RK Store, we offer a wide range of high-quality products to meet your daily needs. Our grocery selection includes fresh vegetables, fruits, rice, oil, soap, spices, and more. We also provide stationery items for students, such as books, pens, pencils, A4 paper, and instrument boxes. Our commitment is to bring you the best products at great prices.

Anybody who lived near Kariavattom Jn. would have bought products from RK Store at least once. Whether you are a student looking to buy things for college or one who wants to buy some groceries, we've got you covered.
